## How to choose

- **Want the "wow" factor?** Pick **Cerebras**. Sub-second responses are impressive and easy to demo.
- **Do a lot of coding or data work?** Pick **Mistral**. Codestral is purpose-built for code.
- **Work with long documents or big datasets?** Pick **Google AI Studio**. Million-token context window.
- **Want to try open-source models?** Pick **Groq**. Runs Llama and other open models fast.

**There is no wrong answer.** Different companies acquire different specialists based on their strategy. That's the point.
